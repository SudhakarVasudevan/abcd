Who We Are

Regenerative Science. Surgical Innovation. Proven Clinical Performance.

SURGICOLL-MESH is an advanced implantable, bioresorbable, tissue-regenerative Type-I collagen matrix developed to support modern reconstructive and regenerative surgical procedures. Backed by patented American technology and manufactured under WHO-GMP and ISO 13485:2016 quality standards, SURGICOLL-MESH® combines biomedical innovation with clinically proven healing performance.

Its ultra-pure Type-I collagen technology promotes rapid tissue regeneration, neo-vascularization, pain reduction, and accelerated healing outcomes — enabling surgeons to achieve reliable clinical performance across complex surgical conditions.

Surgicoll Mesh is an implantable, bioresorbable, tissue-regenerative sterile Type-I collagen matrix designed for advanced surgical reinforcement, tissue repair, and regenerative procedures across multiple medical specialties.

Surgicoll® Mesh is used in a wide range of surgical applications including:

  • Soft tissue & muscle flap reinforcement
  • Hernia repair
  • Neurosurgery
  • Cardiovascular repair
  • Oral & maxillofacial surgery
  • Diabetic ulcer treatment
  • Pelvic floor reconstruction
  • Burn and wound management

Surgicoll Mesh is made from high-purity native un-crosslinked Type-I collagen with over 97% purity. It is free from immunogenic proteins, lipids, and elastin, helping improve biocompatibility and tissue regeneration. The product also uses patented phosphorylation technology to enhance bioactivity and healing performance.

Surgicoll Mesh promotes rapid granulation tissue formation, stimulates blood capillary growth, and supports tissue regeneration within 4 to 5 days after application. Clinical studies also showed reduced inflammation and improved healing response.
